## Overview

The Match process is designed to find the most closely matching record or records from the relevant reference data source to the supplied componentized input data.

## Relevant Options

- SuppressAdditionFields
- MaxResults
- RangeDecompose
- VerifyMatchRules ([click here](/support/match-rules-syntax/) for syntax information)

[Click here](/available-processes/) for the full list of supported options and their descriptions.
